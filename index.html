Who We Are

Born from a love of
wild places

ECO LOVE was founded to provide awareness to people all over the world that caring for the planet isn't a sacrifice — it's a joy. Our ecosystem approach connects forests, oceans, soils, and cities into one living conversation.

We partner with local governments, indigenous communities, and research institutions to turn passion into measurable, lasting change.

Our Mission →
Our Mission

Three pillars of
planetary care

🌿

Protect

Safeguarding critical ecosystems — rainforests, wetlands, coral reefs — against deforestation, pollution, and climate disruption.

🔄

Restore

Rehabilitating damaged lands and waterways through science-led regeneration programs in over 40 countries.

🤝

Empower

Equipping communities with the knowledge, tools, and funding to lead their own conservation futures.
